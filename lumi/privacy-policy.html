Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify You. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Email address
  • Usage Data

Photos and Facial Data

Lumi is a beauty discovery application that analyzes facial features. When You use our Service:

  • Photo Upload: You may upload selfies or photos for analysis. These photos are processed to provide You with beauty insights and recommendations.
  • Facial Analysis: Our Service uses AI technology to analyze facial features such as facial symmetry, skin tone, facial proportions, and other beauty-related characteristics.
  • Local Processing: Facial analysis is performed locally on Your device whenever possible. Photos are not stored on our servers unless explicitly required for Service functionality.
  • No Biometric Identification: We do not use facial data for identification purposes. The analysis is used solely to provide personalized beauty insights and recommendations.

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. This data may include information such as Your Device's Internet Protocol address (IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that You visit, the time and date of Your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

When You access the Service by or through a mobile device, We may collect information automatically, such as the type of mobile device You use, its unique ID, the IP address, mobile operating system, browser type, and other diagnostic data.

Gamification Data

To provide the gamified experience, We collect and store:

  • XP (experience points) and level progress
  • Streak information (consecutive daily usage)
  • Badges and achievements earned
  • Daily challenge completion status
  • Feature discovery progress
